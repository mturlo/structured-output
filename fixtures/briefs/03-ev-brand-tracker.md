# Brief: EV brand tracker — wave 4

**Client:** Mid-tier European auto OEM
**Goal:** Track unaided and aided brand awareness, consideration, and purchase intent for EV models. Wave 4 of an ongoing tracker.

## Audience

Adults 25–65 in DE/FR/UK/ES who plan to buy/lease a vehicle in the next 24 months. Nationally representative quotas. Target n=2000 (500 per market).

## Question sketch

Screener block first — single-choice on country of residence (DE, FR, UK, ES, other → terminate). Then single-choice on next-purchase timeframe (under 6 months, 6–12, 12–24, 24+, no plan). If "24+" or "no plan", terminate.

Unaided awareness: open-ended question, "When you think of electric vehicle brands, which come to mind?" — text field, no length cap (we'll code later).

Aided awareness: multi-choice with the full brand list: Tesla, BYD, VW, Renault, Hyundai, Kia, Stellantis (Peugeot/Citroen/Fiat), Polestar, our brand, none of these. "None of these" exclusive.

Consideration grid: rows = the same brand list (minus "none"), columns = "would definitely consider" / "might consider" / "would not consider" / "don't know enough".

Purchase intent: single-choice — "If you were buying an EV in the next 12 months, which brand would you most likely pick?" Same brand list, single answer.

Brand attribute grid: rows = "innovative", "trustworthy", "good value", "premium", "sustainable". Columns = the top 5 brands from the aided list. Same wave-over-wave so we can trend.

Demographics at the end: age band, gender, household income band, market.
